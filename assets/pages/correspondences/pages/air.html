Air: Element and elemental of nature

25th February 2021 - by Andrieli

Air

Air is an essential element for our life; without it, we’d die. Something that flows of tranquil form as creativity, mind/thought, intuition, and knowledge represent it.

The cardeal wind of Air is the East: hot and damp, like lightness and relaxation. The Season is Spring (time of flourish and the intensity of flowers smell). The day period is dawn/sunrise (between 5:00 and 7:00), in which the atmosphere is clean and peaceful. Colors of this element are associated with Spring flourish, happy tones, like yellow, orange, and light blue (usually pastel color).

Senses connected to it are smell and hearing, both referring to communication with the external world. Therefore, we may remember that Air transmits ancestry (the same oxygen that our ancestors breathed is ours too).

You can connect with Air through breeze and winds. Exhale and inspire; to feel it around your nostrils and bronchi.

Sylphs and Fairies – the elementals of Air

The sylphs are elementals of air, so they can call “air’s fairies”. These souls are quiet and playful beings. Like gnomes, they like to play and hide things. Fairies help in air purification, contribute to our thought, intuition, and creativity. In the same instant that they may be tranquility and fresh breeze, may be destructive hurricanes too.

When the sylphs present in us are imbalanced might cause an excess of creativity and imagination, removing the division between the material and intelligible world (the inverse is also possible).

We may connect with fairy beings going to high mountains, burning incenses, or feeling the wind. They like so much magick offerings with alcoholic drinks (like beer and wine), sweets, fruits, and vegetables. Always remember to talk to them with care and then thank them.
